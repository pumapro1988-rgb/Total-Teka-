However, he was destroyed by a magic organization that sees his existence as dangerous. 200 years later, he is reincarnated as the second son of an aristocrat and obtains the strongest magic power.

“Let’s pretend to be a human this time”

However, being a human lover, he didn’t particularly think of revenge and has decided to pretend to be human in this life. In his past life, he was the guardian deity of a region, and in this life, he’s a local aristocrat, but he does not know common sense. He doesn’t know how to take it easy.
