Akira Kamishiro was a very successful manga author, who was diagnosed with blood cancer one day. He regretted the way he lived, where he could only build up empty relationships. Angry at his mother for asking him to send her some money, he died in a car accident. However, he was given a chance to choose his new mother and reincarnate in another world.

Akira, not wanting to have a repeat of his past life, decided to choose an ordinary but safe life. He is reincarnated as Akira Rainford, the only son of a magic store owner, with his previous life's memories. He had no talent for swordsmanship or magic, but he doesn't care as he was still loved by his family. At one point, he realized that he can embody various things by "drawing a picture". That's when he noticed his cheat ability...!?

This is Akira's new story who wants to live a mediocre life in another world and the adventures he embarks on to protect his peaceful place!!
